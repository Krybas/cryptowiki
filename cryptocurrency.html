What is cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography, which makes it nearly impossible to counterfeit or double-spend. Most cryptocurrencies exist on decentralized networks using blockchain technology a distributed ledger enforced by a disparate network of computers.

    Key features of cryptocurrency

  • Cryptocurrency is a decentralized digital asset, not controlled by governments.
  • Advantages:
    • Cheaper and faster money transfers.
    • Decentralized, reducing risk of single point failures.
  • Disadvantages:
    • Price volatility.
    • High energy consumption for mining.
    • Association with criminal activities.
  • Experts predict blockchain could disrupt industries like finance and law.

About most popular cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by an unknown person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. Since then, thousands of alternative cryptocurrencies have been created, including Ethereum, Ripple, and Litecoin.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that enables developers to build and deploy smart contracts, which are self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. The native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum network is called Ether (ETH).

Binance Coin

Binance Coin is a cryptocurrency used to pay for fees on the Binance cryptocurrency exchange. The coin was launched in 2017 and is used to facilitate transactions on the Binance platform, as well as to participate in token sales hosted on the Binance Launchpad.

Cardano

Cardano is a blockchain platform that aims to provide a more secure and scalable infrastructure for the development of decentralized applications and smart contracts. The native cryptocurrency of the Cardano network is called ADA.

Solana

Solana is a high-performance blockchain platform that aims to provide fast, secure, and scalable infrastructure for decentralized applications and crypto-currencies. The native cryptocurrency of the Solana network is called SOL.

Ripple XRP

Ripple is a digital payment protocol that enables fast, low-cost cross-border transactions. The native cryptocurrency of the Ripple network is called XRP.
